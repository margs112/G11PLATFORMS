USHARE +

USHARE is an inclusive platform that allows students to transcend their presence from just the seats and stage to something bigger — where they get to be part of the team behind the scenes. This will give students the opportunity to sign up as ushers and volunteers to work hand in hand with the SCB officers in bringing their events to life. USHARE does not only offer students bigger roles; but bringing them closer to where they truly belong.

LAKAN AT LAKAMBINI +

Haven’t had enough of Diyos at Diyosa? SENTRO presents you: Lakan at Lakambini ng La Salle Lipa! With this proposition, the SCB ought to take ownership of the pageantry concept, making it into an anticipated Lipasallian tradition. The crown of Lakan and Lakambini ng La Salle Lipa will be rolling, such that every iteration of the pageant will have different hosts: it will start with the SHS, the JHS, and then, the GS, before the cycle repeats—with the previous winner crowning the next.

inSCriBe +

inSCRiBe is designed to be the official website of the Senior Student Coordinating Board. It shall serve as a digital archive that documents every affair of the SSCB, displays the profiles of incumbent SSCB officers and their offices, and a platform for classes to send in their concerns anytime. It encourages a culture of leadership that is swift, advanced, and beneficially transparent.

VOICE AND VOTE +

Voice and Vote is a one-day event that aims to promote political literacy and responsible citizenship among students. In light of the upcoming Sangguniang Kabataan and Barangay Elections, this initiative immerses students in mock electoral voting, scenario-based challenges, and engaging discussions that will help them critically assess political candidates, spot misinformation, and understand the power of informed participation in the democratic process. More than just a simulation, Voice and Vote serves as a platform to better understand the realities of our society and stand for truth equipping them to become socially aware, politically conscious, and grounded in values that will guide us as future leaders and citizens.

LA SALLE 'YAN EH! +

What better way to make a difference than through giving back? This student-led charity project encourages every class to donate food, hygiene kits, and clothing. Partnering with the local Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) or community groups, the team goes straight to where help is needed most. Not only does this build class spirit, but it also sparks leadership, teamwork, and a deeper connection with the community. It’s hands-on. It’s heartfelt. And it’s one way we show what being Lasallian truly means.
